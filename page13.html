Why Signing Employment Contracts During Mercury Retrograde Might Be a Bad Idea

Astrology-based—use at your own discretion

Did you know that Mercury Retrograde, an astrological phenomenon, can affect communication, technology, and even business transactions? Although some may consider it mere superstition, many believe that it can impact important aspects of life. For instance, it is commonly advised to exercise caution when signing crucial documents such as employment contracts, lease agreements, and business deals during Mercury retrograde periods. It is better to be safe than sorry, so be mindful of this astrological event and take the necessary precautions to protect yourself and your business.


What is Mercury Retrograde?

Mercury retrograde usually happens 3 times a year, lasting approximately 3 weeks every time. During this period, astrologers caution that the influence of Mercury can lead to misunderstandings, delays, regret, and communication errors. This phenomenon is said to affect not only verbal and written communication but also technological devices and travel plans. You don’t have to believe in astrology, remember, there is free will, and it affects everyone differently. I have experienced making huge decisions during this time, which included starting a new business that failed months later, moving to a new apartment that I was not happy about, buying a sound bar that broke, and, oh yes, signing that contract!!! And guess what I wasn't happy in that role at all and resigned a few months later.


The Impact on Employment Contracts:

An employment contract is a crucial commitment for both employers and employees. It lays out the expectations of employment, such as job duties, pay, perks, and other relevant information. During the Mercury retrograde period, there is a higher likelihood of miscommunication and misunderstandings. As a result, it may not be the best time to enter into such agreements..

Reasons to Avoid Signing Employment Contracts During Mercury Retrograde:

Misunderstandings – Mercury retrograde is often associated with confusion and misinterpretation. This could lead to ambiguities in the contract language or miscommunication regarding terms and conditions, potentially causing issues down the line.


Delays and Revisions – Contracts signed during Mercury retrograde may be subject to delays and revisions. What seemed clear and straightforward at the time of signing could later require extensive modifications or renegotiations due to overlooked details or unforeseen complications. This can result in frustration, wasted time, and extra expenses.

Unforeseen Changes – Mercury retrograde is notorious for bringing unexpected changes and disruptions. Signing an employment contract during this time could increase the likelihood of unforeseen circumstances arising that impact the terms of employment or the stability of the company.



Technology Glitches – In today’s digital age, many employment contracts are signed electronically. Mercury's retrograde influence on technology may heighten the risk of glitches or errors in the electronic signing process, potentially leading to incomplete or invalid contracts.

Long-Term Implications – Employment contracts often have long-term implications for both parties involved. Making such a significant commitment during a period of cosmic unrest may not bode well for the future stability and success of the employment relationship.

Regrettable Decisions: Retrograde periods are notorious for clouding judgment and fostering a sense of impulsivity. What may seem like a good idea during Mercury retrograde could later be regretted once clarity is restored. Avoid making hasty decisions, especially those with long-term consequences, and take the time to carefully evaluate all options before committing to any contract.

Financial Losses: Mercury retrograde can bring about unexpected financial setbacks and losses. Signing a contract during this period may expose you to risks that you hadn’t anticipated, such as hidden fees, unfavourable terms, or unforeseen circumstances that impact your financial stability. Proceeding with caution during the Mercury retrograde can help mitigate these risks and protect your financial interests.
